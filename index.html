FAQ

Common questions

Do you charge a fee for your service?

Yes, we charge a transparent flat fee for our car-buying service, which we discuss upfront before you commit. This fee covers sourcing, negotiation, inspections, history checks, and paperwork handling. Many customers find the fee pays for itself through the savings we negotiate—plus the time you save. Finance enquiries and initial consultations are always free.

What areas do you service?

We're based in Melbourne and service all of metropolitan Melbourne and regional Victoria. We can arrange delivery to your door anywhere in the state. For interstate sourcing (finding a car from NSW, QLD, etc.), we can assist on a case-by-case basis—just ask.

How long does it take to source a car?

For common vehicles (popular SUVs, utes, sedans), we typically have a shortlist ready within 24–48 hours. Rarer vehicles or very specific requirements may take longer—we'll give you a realistic timeframe upfront. Once you've chosen a car, settlement and delivery usually happens within the same week.

Can you organise finance pre-approval?

Absolutely. Pre-approval is a smart move—it means you know your budget and can move quickly when the right car comes up. We compare rates from 30+ lenders and can usually get conditional pre-approval within 24 hours. This service is available whether you're buying through us or sourcing your own vehicle.

Do you buy trade-ins or help sell my current car?

Yes to both. If you're buying a car through us, we can handle your trade-in and factor it into the deal. If you just want to sell, we can give you a fair market valuation and either purchase it directly or sell it through our dealer network. No tyre-kickers, no weekend inspections, no hassle.

Where do you source vehicles from?

We search everywhere: franchised dealers, independent yards, dealer-only auctions, and private sales. This wide net means we often find vehicles that aren't publicly advertised yet. Every vehicle we recommend gets a full history check (PPSR, odometer, write-off register) and either a physical inspection or detailed photo/video assessment.

What documents do I need for finance?

Generally you'll need: photo ID (driver's licence), proof of income (recent payslips or tax returns if self-employed), and bank statements from the last 90 days. Requirements vary by lender and loan type—we'll tell you exactly what's needed once we've assessed your situation. ABN holders and business buyers may have additional requirements.

Is there any obligation if I enquire?

None at all. Your initial enquiry and consultation are completely free and no-obligation. We'll have a chat about what you're after, give you an idea of what's out there, and explain how we work. You only commit (and pay our fee) when you decide to proceed with us finding or financing a vehicle.
